Anatomy of Boba Milk Tea

Fat Straw:

 Used to help you drink your boba! Optionally, if you didn’t get any boba or add-on’s, then a skinny straw can be used as well. NOTE: It’s best to wait a bit after your drink has just been sealed, that way it’s easier to poke through!

Vacuum Sealed Plastic Cover:

 Used to cover your drink and help prevent anything from spilling. Before poking your straw into it, it’s safe enough to even turn your drink completely upside down and no milk tea will leak!

Milk Tea:

 Tea and milk mixture! The classic milk tea typically uses black tea, but you can always opt to have green tea instead! If trying a flavor other than classic, you can choose from many different flavorings of fruity to sweet syrups or fresh fruit makes a large combination of flavors to choose from!

Cup Label:

 Used to denote the location’s name and brand. The nicer the cup label, the more credible the location is.

Boba:

 AKA “tapiocal pearls”
